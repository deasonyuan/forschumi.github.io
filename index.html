Short Biography

Currently, I am a Ph.D. student in Electronic Science and Technology at Shenzhen Graduate School, Harbin Institute of Technology.

Before that, in September 2014, I was recommended to pursue for a Ph.D. degree in Harbin Institute of Technology without entrance examination. In the Microelectronics and SoC Research Center, I started my investigation from unmanned aerial vehicles controller design and medical computed tomography analysis, and finished my Bachelor thesis in May 2015 and received my B.S. degree in Physics in June 2015. As the last generation of the center, I worked under the supervision of Professor Yan Zhang. Shortly after, my interests had been diverted to the computational nature of vision. After the closure of the center in December 2017, I collaborated with Professor Liyi Xiao from the School of Astronautics in Harbin Institute of Technology and Associate Professor Fei Wang from the Shenzhen Graduate School in Harbin Institute of Technology. After that, I want to solve some real-world problems, such as object detection and tracking. Now, I am interested in the deep structural modeling of visual object tracking.

Research Interest

My research interests include: Computer Vision and Computer Architecture.
Especially, I am focusing on:

  • High performance visual object tracking;
  • CNN model compression and acceleration;
  • FPGA-based acceleration systems for deep learning based applications.
